NINA WEDDING SINGER

Hi, I’m Nina and I love music and weddings! Having a job where I get to bring two of my favourite things together is a joy and I would be delighted to help make your special day a time you’ll never forget, for all the right reasons!

Nina presents the perfect entertainment for your wedding or function - whether you'd like a filled dancefloor for your evening party, a moving and memorable ceremony or a toe tapping drinks reception, her fantastic vocals, years of experience and friendly professionalism will keep your guests dancing the night away... …or wiping a tear away in the aisle!

Nina is a successful session singer who has worked for Dannii Minogue, Ronan Keating and Girls Aloud among others. Her sound system and lighting are second to none, she has a fantastic voice and extensive repertoire and for a fraction of the price of a band and DJ, you can rest assured that your live music is in safe hands.

Nina covers the whole of the south and south east area; Surrey, Kent, East and West Sussex, Hampshire, Berkshire, Bucks and beyond. She has recently won a Wedding Industry Award for music and is the recommended supplier to several wonderful venues. Her equipment is PAT tested, she has public liability insurance and is a Musicians' Union member.
